About

Stephy Zaviour is an Indian costume designer from Kerala, known for her extensive work in Malayalam cinema.She started her career as costumer designer in the industry when she was just 23. Within two years, she completed around 16 films, including Lord Livingstone 7000 Kandi, Guppy and Ezra.In 2016, Stephy won her first Kerala State Award for Costume Designer for her work in the movie Guppy :)
